# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 8, 2026

FLIP is built and operated by **Izhan Ansari** ("we", "us"). This policy explains what happens — and, more importantly, what *doesn't* happen — with your data when you use the FLIP app on iOS.

## TL;DR

**FLIP collects nothing about you.** No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, no servers. The app runs entirely on your device.

## What we collect

Nothing.

FLIP does not collect, transmit, store, sell, share, rent, or otherwise process any personal information, usage data, device identifiers, location data, contacts, photos, health data, or any other information about you. There is no sign-up, no login, and no profile.

## What FLIP uses on your device

The app uses a small number of standard iOS capabilities, all locally:

- **Motion sensor (CoreMotion):** to detect when you flip or tilt the phone to start a deep-work session or take a break. Motion data is read in real time on-device and never stored or transmitted. iOS will ask for your permission the first time motion is used.
- **Notifications:** scheduled locally on your device when a timer is running, so you get a chime when the phase ends. No remote / push notifications. iOS will ask for your permission the first time.
- **Live Activity (ActivityKit):** the lock-screen banner and Dynamic Island countdown are rendered locally by iOS using parameters from the app. No push tokens are used; no servers are involved.
- **Sound and haptics:** standard iOS audio and Taptic Engine APIs. No data leaves the device.
- **Spotlight indexing (CoreSpotlight):** the app registers itself in your device's local search index so you can find FLIP by typing related terms (e.g. "timer", "hourglass"). The index lives entirely on your device.

None of these capabilities cause data to leave your phone.

## Network access

FLIP does not make any network requests of any kind. The app does not contact our servers, third-party servers, or any remote service.

## Third parties

FLIP does not include third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising frameworks, crash reporters, A/B testing platforms, or remote configuration services.

## Children's privacy

Because FLIP collects no information at all, it is suitable for users of any age. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is nothing to access, correct, port, restrict, object to, or delete. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored timer state from your device.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever update FLIP in a way that changes this policy (for example, adding optional iCloud sync), we will update the effective date at the top and post the new version at the same URL before that release ships.
